OPERATORS IN JAVASCRIPTS

= Assignment operator
== Equal to (checks for the value only) i.e console.log(2==2)
=== exactly equals to (checks for the value and datatypes) i.e console.log('2'===2)
+ Addition/Concatination 
- subtraction
* multiplication
/ division
** Raise to the power
++ Increament
-- Decrement
> greater than
< less than
>= greater than or equal to
<= less than or equal to
!= not equal to
% = (modulus) returns remainder afer dividing two numbers
